🔍 While all eyes are on the Middle East, the most significant opportunity may be quietly forming in Southeast Asia.

The March 2 drone strikes that put literal holes in AWS data centers across the UAE and Bahrain shattered hyperscalers' confidence in the Gulf as stable digital real estate. When the Middle East is no longer safe, who becomes the next digital hub?

This war, rather than slowing that momentum, may accelerate it — forcing capital that had planned Gulf infrastructure to fast-track Malaysia as a primary or backup zone. This is the structural opportunity most observers are missing beneath the war noise.

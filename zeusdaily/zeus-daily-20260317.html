78岁DoorDash外卖员被摄像头记录，网友集资近100万美元——一段门铃摄像头拍到78岁老人深夜送餐的视频在网上疯传，美国网友自发募款，最终筹集近100万美元赠予这位老人和他的妻子。这位老人表示从未想到会有如此回应，这笔钱将让他们不再需要为生计奔波。
78-year-old DoorDash driver goes viral; strangers donate nearly $1 million. A Ring camera video of an elderly man delivering food went viral, prompting Americans to donate nearly $1M to him and his wife. The couple said they never expected such an outpouring.
03
马大（UPM）喷雾疫苗或改变全球山羊绵羊接种方式——UPM研究团队经数十年研究开发出一种喷雾型疫苗，有效替代传统针剂，据称可大幅降低畜牧业接种成本与动物应激反应。马来西亚科研成果再登国际舞台。
UPM spray vaccine could transform global goat & sheep vaccination. Universiti Putra Malaysia's spray-type vaccine, developed over decades, could replace traditional injections, significantly reducing livestock vaccination costs and animal stress. Malaysian research gets global recognition.
04
比特币荣登"2026年初最差表现资产"——黄金和石油笑了——CoinGecko报告指出，2026年初黄金和原油以惊人涨幅领跑所有资产类别，而比特币成为同期表现最差的主要资产，年初至今跌幅一度超过23%。这种反差在2021年本是不可想象的场景。世道变了。
Bitcoin crowned "worst performing major asset" of early 2026 as gold and oil lead all classes. CoinGecko data shows crude oil and gold leading all asset classes in early 2026, with BTC down over 23% YTD at its worst. A reversal of the 2021 narrative that would have seemed unimaginable just years ago.
05
伊朗互联网管控下，Starlink用户靠卫星信号与外界联系——伊朗政府在战争爆发后实施全面互联网封锁，但Fox News记者在推文发出后数分钟内便通过Starlink联系到德黑兰用户。讽刺的是：美国正在轰炸伊朗，同时美国公司的卫星正在帮伊朗人看轰炸新闻。
Iran's internet is blacked out — but Starlink users are still online. Minutes after a Fox News reporter asked for Starlink contacts in Iran, users in Tehran and western Iran responded. The irony: US forces are bombing Iran while a US company's satellites are helping Iranians watch news of the bombing.
06
油价破百，韩国30年来首次对燃油设定价格上限——首尔汽油零售价上限定为每升1,724韩元（约$1.17），柴油1,713韩元，每两周根据国际市场重新计算一次。上一次韩国动用这项机制，还是1990年代亚洲金融危机期间。马来西亚的燃油补贴机制目前承受同方向的压力，只是还没到这一步。
South Korea caps fuel prices for the first time since the 1990s. Gasoline capped at 1,724 KRW/litre (~$1.17), diesel at 1,713 KRW, recalculated every two weeks against international markets. The last time Seoul used this mechanism was the Asian Financial Crisis. Malaysia's fuel subsidy system is under the same directional pressure — just hasn't reached this point yet.
